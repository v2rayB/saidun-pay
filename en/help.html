Common issues with Seton VPN: 1 IOS connection frequently disconnects

Reason: iOS devices themselves have a 15M NE memory limit, and when connecting to a VPN for use, VPN disconnection may occur when the limit exceeds 15M.
Solution:
1. It is recommended to upgrade the iOS system to iOS 15, as this will increase the memory limit from 15M to 50M
2. Clean up programs that are not used in the background. If there are photos on the phone that are automatically synchronized or updated in the app store, turn off these settings as well
PS: The iOS 15M NE memory issue is a limitation of Apple itself.

After a successful connection, TikTok still cannot be used?

Note: Hong Kong, India, and Canada are still unable to view the three regions due to Tiktok's cessation of operations.
Access method of overseas Tiktok: turn off the positioning function of Bluetooth and the system, pull out the Sim card of the mobile phone, change the time zone into the United States and the language into English in the universal mobile phone, then restart the mobile phone, open the VPN and select the United States and global mode to access overseas Tiktok

Images cannot be flashed/messages cannot be displayed

Reason: Currently, 5G networks/signals around the world are unstable, which directly leads to devices switching networks multiple times in a short period of time, resulting in the inability to load the required viewing content and extremely poor user experience.
Solution:
1. Turn off the 5G function of iPhone or iPad (this setting is not required when using Wi Fi)
2. Operation steps: System settings → Cellular network → Cellular data options → Voice and data → 4G

IOS 16 system connection prompt small issue

PS: Various bugs in the iOS 16 system itself

Does Seton VPN restrict traffic?

Seton VPN has no traffic restrictions, but please note that if your device is not connected to the internet, you cannot use any VPN software.
